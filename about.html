About Me

Hey there, I'm Mustafa, and I live in the Chicagoland area. I started doing photography a few years ago, and I really love taking pictures of special moments. I've tried a few different genres of photography, but I really enjoy taking pictures of people on the street, at weddings, and in portrait style. Photography lets you see the world from other people's perspectives and also helps you learn new things. That's why I think it's a great idea for others to give it a try too. If you have any questions or just want to talk, you can email me or send me a message on Instagram. I'm excited to help you out and show you the photos I've taken!
